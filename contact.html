Frequently Asked Questions

Are you available for research collaborations?

Yes, I'm always interested in research collaborations, especially in areas related to reinforcement learning, evolutionary computing, and machine learning applications. Please reach out via email with details about your proposed collaboration.

Do you supervise student projects?

I currently supervise several student projects at both undergraduate and graduate levels. If you're interested in working with me, please send me an email with your research interests and background.

Can I access your research code and datasets?

Most of my research code is available on GitHub. You can find links to the repositories in my publications and projects pages. For datasets, please contact me directly as some may have usage restrictions.

What are your current research interests?

My current research focuses on multi-agent reinforcement learning, evolutionary algorithms for neural architecture search, and hybrid optimization methods. I'm particularly interested in applications to distributed systems and financial markets.
