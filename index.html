About Me

I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science at University of Galway, Ireland. My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning and Evolutionary Computing. I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science, and a dual Bachelor's in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics from China.

My academic interests lie in intelligent optimization, deep reinforcement learning, and real-world applications of machine learning. I aim to build adaptive AI systems inspired by natural evolution and learning dynamics.

Research Interests

  • Reinforcement Learning (PPO, SAC, DQN)
  • Evolutionary Algorithms (GA, PSO, SA)
  • Machine Learning and Deep Learning
  • Data Science and Applied Statistics
